Privacy Policy

Effective Date:

Overview

Debts Highlighter is a Chrome extension that lets you highlight specific words, phrases, and small numeric values (0–249) on web pages. All processing happens locally on your device. No personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted to any external server.

Data We Access

Website Content: visible text on the web pages you visit, only for the purpose of applying highlights that you configure.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect: personally identifiable information, financial or payment data, authentication credentials, location, browsing history, or user activity such as clicks or keystrokes.

Data Storage

The extension stores only your highlight groups and phrases, highlight colors, toggle (on/off) state, and a local password for the popup. This information is saved in chrome.storage.local within your browser and never leaves your device.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties.

Security

Because all data is processed locally and not sent over the internet, the risk of interception or misuse is minimized.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the “Effective Date” and publish the new version at the same URL.
